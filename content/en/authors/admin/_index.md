Dr. Sisi Li is a Professor at the **International Cancer Center, Medical School, Shenzhen University**, where she serves as a doctoral supervisor. She received her PhD from **Fudan University** and completed her postdoctoral training at **Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center** in New York.

Dr. Li is a recipient of the **National Young Talent Program (Category B)**, the **Shenzhen Outstanding Youth Foundation**, and the **Shenzhen Overseas High-Level Talent** designation. She currently serves as a council member of the *Shenzhen Federation of Western-Returned Scholars* and as a committee member of the *Basic Medical Sciences Branch of the Chinese Society of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology*.

## Research

Her laboratory investigates the molecular mechanisms underlying **RNA homeostasis regulation**. Integrating biochemistry, molecular biology, and cell biology, the group has established a comprehensive research framework that spans:

- **Chemical and structural analysis** of regulatory RNAs
- **Elucidation of RNA–protein regulatory mechanisms** governing transcript stability and function
- **In vivo validation** of physiological roles in development and disease
- **Exploration of RNA-targeted therapeutic strategies**, with a particular focus on immune-related disorders

## Publications & Recognition

Over the past five years, Dr. Li has published more than ten papers as first or corresponding author (including co-corresponding) in journals such as ***Science***, ***Nature Communications***, ***Nature Chemical Biology***, ***PNAS***, and ***Molecular Plant***. Several of her studies have been highlighted by accompanying commentaries and featured in academic media. She serves as a reviewer for multiple internationally recognized journals and is the principal investigator of numerous national and provincial research grants from the **NSFC**, **Shenzhen Municipal Science and Technology Innovation Commission**, and other agencies.
